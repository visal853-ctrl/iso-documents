# Information Security Policy

## Purpose
The purpose of this Information Security Policy is to safeguard the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information managed by EuroTrust Bank AG in compliance with ISO 27001:2022.

## Scope
This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties who manage or access information belonging to EuroTrust Bank AG.

## Objectives
- Protect sensitive and critical information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.
- Ensure compliance with all relevant laws and regulations related to information security.
- Promote a culture of information security awareness among all staff.

## Roles and Responsibilities
- **Management:** Ensure the implementation of the Information Security Management System (ISMS) and provide necessary resources.
- **Information Security Officer:** Oversee the information security framework and report to management on its effectiveness.
- **Employees:** Adhere to the security policies, practices, and applicable laws.

## Security Controls
1. **Access Control:** All access to information is controlled and monitored. Authentication mechanisms must be in place.
2. **Data Encryption:** Sensitive data must be encrypted at rest and in transit.
3. **Incident Management:** A formal process for managing information security incidents including identification, response, and reporting.
4. **Training and Awareness:** Regular security training sessions for all employees to recognize and mitigate security threats.
5. **Risk Assessment:** Conduct periodic risk assessments to identify and mitigate vulnerabilities and threats.

## Compliance
EuroTrust Bank AG will ensure compliance with ISO 27001:2022 and regularly review this policy to ensure its effectiveness and relevance to the organization.

## Review and Revision
This policy will be reviewed on an annual basis and updated as necessary to respond to new security challenges or changes in legislation.
